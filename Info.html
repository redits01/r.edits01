About Me

Editor & Graphic Designer

Behind this account is an average high school student passionate about photo editing and graphic design. I enjoy creating automotive‑themed wallpapers and visual content for social media.

Tools I Use

  • Lightroom (Mobile & Desktop)

How I Got Started

I started photographing cars in 2020. My first edits a year later were rough, so I paused. In late 2024, I rebooted—learning from YouTube and Instagram creators. I launched this Instagram, then expanded into wallpapers, editing contests, and Reels. The community’s support has kept me going—I’m truly thankful!
